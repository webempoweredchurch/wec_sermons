Meeting Jesus in Mark

Lenten Sermon Series

March 5- April 16

The Gospel of Mark was the first Gospel written about the life of Jesus. In its pages, we find a Jesus who is complex and mysterious, yet easily approached and compassionate. During the season of Lent, as we prepare for Easter, we will look at Jesus through the eyes of Mark. Who we find there will surprise and challenge us, no matter how long we have known him! Join us as we meet Jesus, for the first time or hundreth time, in the book of Mark.
