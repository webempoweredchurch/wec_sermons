GROUP IMPACT

A study sheet for further exploration
March 5, 2006

conversions: from insulation to intimacy

By: Mike Slaughter

Reread Genesis 12
  1. Name a way you insulate yourself from problems or pain in your relationships.
  2. What are some of the shared values and visions you have or want to have in your marriage?
  3. If you are married, what is the biggest need for conversion as it relates to intimacy with your spouse? If you are single, as it relates to celibacy in your dedication to God?
  4. Describe the conversion that needs to take place in your life.
  5. How is Gods promise of blessing being fulfilled in your closest relationships?

Action Steps:

How will you begin to fully commit to Gods promises through intimacy?

  • By honoring your spouse and marriage in true godly commitment
  • By committing to God and self to only marrying a person with whom you can be equally yoked
  • By preparing yourself for a deep spirit-to-spirit relationship through celibacy
  • By communicating encouragement, feeling and value to your spouse
  • By celebrating your marriage relationship with gratitude and touch
